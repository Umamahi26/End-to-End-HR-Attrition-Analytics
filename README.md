# End-to-End HR Attrition Analytics Project

## 📊 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition patterns using **Excel, SQL, and Power BI** to identify key workforce risks and support strategic HR decision-making.

The dashboard provides actionable insights on employee satisfaction, compensation trends, promotion impact, and work-life balance factors influencing attrition.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations face significant productivity and financial losses due to high employee attrition.
This project aims to:

* Identify major attrition drivers
* Detect high-risk employee segments
* Support HR leadership with data-driven retention strategies

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

* Excel → Data Cleaning & Pre-processing
* SQL → Exploratory Data Analysis & Business Queries
* Power BI → Interactive Dashboard & KPI Visualization

---

## 📁 Dataset Description

The dataset includes employee-level information such as:

* Job Role
* Monthly Income
* Job Satisfaction
* Work-Life Balance
* Years at Company
* Promotions
* Performance Rating
* Attrition Status

---

## 📌 Key KPIs Built

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate
* Average Income
* Average Job Satisfaction

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

✔ Employees with **0–2 years experience** show highest attrition risk

✔ **Low salary slab employees** contribute majorly to attrition

✔ Lack of **promotions and recognition** increases resignation probability

✔ Poor **work-life balance** correlates with higher exits

✔ Certain job roles like **Technology & Healthcare** show higher attrition concentration

---

## 🧠 SQL Analysis Highlights

* Attrition Rate Calculation using conditional aggregation
* Salary ranking using Window Functions
* Attrition distribution by job role, income slab, and promotions
* Satisfaction vs attrition correlation analysis

---

## 📊 Power BI Dashboard Features

* Interactive slicers for Work-Life Balance
* Attrition trend by Years at Company
* Income slab vs attrition comparison
* Recognition impact visualization
* Job Satisfaction donut analysis

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Improve onboarding experience for early tenure employees
* Introduce structured promotion cycles
* Implement recognition & reward programs
* Benchmark salary competitiveness
* Monitor burnout signals via work-life balance metrics
